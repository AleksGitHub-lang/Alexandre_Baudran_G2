The Weeknd - Too late

The Weeknd's "Too Late" is a song about the regret and desperation that comes after a relationship has ended. In the first verse, the singer acknowledges that he has made mistakes and led his partner on, and now realizes that he can't live without their love. The chorus reflects the realization that it's too late to save the relationship and they're both on their own in trying to move on from the past.

The World of Hans Zimmer

He is among the most famous composers of original soundtracks, notably the music for Gladiator, Inception, Pearl Harbor and The Dark Knight Rises. During his career, Hans Zimmer has won an Oscar (for The Lion King), two Golden Globes, four Grammy Awards and his nominations number in the dozens. The concert “The World of Hans Zimmer - A New Dimension” invites you to rediscover his most beautiful compositions.
